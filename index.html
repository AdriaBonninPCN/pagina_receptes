Noodle

This ramen plate features a rich, savory broth with a balanced blend of soy and miso, brimming with depth. Nestled inside is a bed of perfectly cooked, springy noodles, topped with tender, marinated slices of chashu pork. Soft-boiled eggs with golden, gooey yolks sit on one side, adding a creamy texture. Each component harmonizes for a comforting and flavorful experience in every bite.
