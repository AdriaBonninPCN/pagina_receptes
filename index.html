Udon

Noodle

This udon bowl offers thick, chewy noodles in a delicate dashi broth, rich with umami. Topped with tender tempura shrimp, fresh green onions, and a sprinkle of bonito flakes, it combines gentle warmth and texture. A hint of ginger adds brightness, creating a simple yet comforting flavor experience.

Ramen

Noodle

This ramen plate features a rich, savory broth with a balanced blend of soy and miso, brimming with depth. Nestled inside is a bed of perfectly cooked, springy noodles, topped with tender, marinated slices of chashu pork. Soft-boiled eggs with golden, gooey yolks sit on one side, adding a creamy texture. Fresh green onions, seaweed, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds add brightness and a hint of crunch, while a touch of spicy chili oil completes the dish with a slight kick. Each component harmonizes for a comforting and flavorful experience in every bite.
